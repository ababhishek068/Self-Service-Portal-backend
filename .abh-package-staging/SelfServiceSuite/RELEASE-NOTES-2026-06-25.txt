SELF SERVICE SUITE — RELEASE NOTES (2026-06-25)
================================================

WHAT IS NEW IN THIS BUILD
-------------------------
- Pending Approval status shows correctly after you request approval (including transfer orders)
- Cancel on list and detail while status is Pending Approval
- Attachments on finance modules: upload on Draft/Open only; view anytime; delete on Draft/Open only
- Imprest advance lines: amount auto-calculated from type, destination and days; manual entry when BC returns no value
- Staff claims: Medical Claim auto-selects Medical Allowance; medical amount + hospital category auto-fill amount and refund
- Petty cash replenishment detail shows paying/receiving accounts and source amount
- Vehicle Transfer marked Coming Soon (disabled in this release)

MODULE GUIDANCE (for users and testers)
---------------------------------------

Finance — Imprest Requisition
  Create the header first, then add advance lines (type, destination, duty area, days, amount).
  Attach documents, then request approval.

Finance — Imprest Surrender
  Select a posted imprest; surrender lines are generated automatically.
  Enter spent and cash receipt details, Save, then request approval.

Finance — Staff Claim
  Create the header, add claim lines, attach receipts, request approval.
  Medical claims: pick Medical Claim, enter medical amount and hospital category for auto amounts.

Finance — Petty Cash Request
  Create the header, add one line per expense; total equals the sum of lines.

Finance — Petty Cash Replenishment
  Set paying and receiving accounts and source amount (receiving amount matches source).
  Submitted to Business Central for inter-bank transfer approval.

Facilities — Purchase Requisition
  Header needs needed-by date and description.
  Each line needs type, number, quantity and purpose; attach specs, then request approval.

Facilities — Store Requisition
  Create the header, add item or asset lines (type, issuing store, item, quantity).
  Attach documents, then request approval.

Facilities — Transfer Orders
  From/to locations and items must exist in Business Central.
  Create the header, add lines, request approval when complete.

Facilities — Transport Requisition
  Trip date cannot be in the past.
  Add passengers after the header, then request approval.

Facilities — Fuel Requisition
  Vehicle fuel needs a registration number; recharge card needs a card number.
  Enter dealer, quantity and price per litre, then request approval.

Facilities — Gate Pass
  Gate passes appear here after the source request exists in Business Central
  (store issue, transfer order, or asset transfer).
  Open gate passes can be sent for approval from the detail screen.
  New asset transfer requests are not available yet (Vehicle Transfer coming soon).

Facilities — Vehicle Transfer
  Coming soon — not available in this release.

HR — Salary Advance
  Percentage cannot exceed 100% of salary. Repayment terms are set in Business Central.

HR — Training Request
  Saved as draft first; review, then request approval.

HR — Document Requisition
  Coming soon — not available in this release.

INSTALL ON CLIENT (C:\TA\SelfServiceSuite\)
-------------------------------------------
1. powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File C:\TA\SelfServiceSuite\SelfServicePortal\deploy\windows\stop-suite.ps1
2. Backup: C:\TA\SelfServiceSuite → C:\TA\SelfServiceSuite-backup
3. Extract ZIP to C:\TA\SelfServiceSuite
4. C:\TA\SelfServiceSuite\SelfServicePortal\deploy\windows\start-suite.bat
5. Open http://10.30.4.23:4000 and press Ctrl+F5 once.

BUSINESS CENTRAL (admin)
------------------------
Enable approval workflows for each document type before users can request approval.
If BC reports "workflow is not configured", activate the matching workflow in Business Central.
Master data (locations, items, accounts, dimensions) must exist in BC; the portal displays what BC returns.
